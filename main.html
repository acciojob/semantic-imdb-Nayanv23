Bhool Bhulaiyaa
Actors Quotes
Bhool Bhulaiyaa (transl. Labyrinth) is a 2007 Indian Hindi-language psychological horror comedy film directed by Priyadarshan. The story follows Dr. Aditya Shrivastav, a psychiatrist who visits an ancestral mansion and uncovers a mystery involving a ghost named Manjulika.
  • Akshay Kumar as Dr. Aditya Shrivastav - The intelligent psychiatrist and hero of the story.
  • Vidya Balan as Avni Chaturvedi / Manjulika - The complex female lead with a haunted past.
  • Shiney Ahuja as Siddharth Chaturvedi - Avni's husband caught in the mystery.
"Ami je tomar" - A hauntingly beautiful song that captures the essence of the film's supernatural romance.
The original "Bhool Bhulaiyaa" movie was released on October 12, 2007. It has an IMDb rating of approximately 7.4/10.
Produced by: Bhushan Kumar
